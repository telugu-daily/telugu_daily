# Telugu Daily — Instagram Ad Script 2

## "వాళ్ళు నవ్వినప్పుడు..." (When they laughed at you...)

**Format:** Instagram Reel / Story | **Duration:** 30 seconds | **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (Vertical)
**Setting:** Home, social hangout, online — NO school scenes

---

## Concept: The Social Media Moment

The pain point shifts from classroom to **online life** — comments, video calls, Instagram itself.
Every Telugu teenager lives on social media. The embarrassment here is more personal and more frequent than school.

---

## Module 1: THE HOOK (0–3 seconds)

**Goal:** Hit the rawest nerve in 2 seconds.

**Scene:**
Girl is recording herself for a Reel/TikTok. She's confident, having fun. She posts it.

Camera cuts to her face as she reads the comments — one comment in English that she doesn't understand. She zooms in, reads it again. Still lost. She screenshots it, pastes it in Google Translate.

**Text overlay flashes:**

> "ఇది నీకు తెలుసా?"
> *(Do you know this feeling?)*

**Why this works:**
Every teenage girl making content online has had this exact moment. It's private, personal, and slightly painful — perfect scroll-stopper on Instagram of all platforms.

**Shoot tip:** Film her phone screen as she reads comments. Use a fake/prop comment — something genuinely confusing like *"your vibe is unmatched bestie fr no cap"*. Her confused face = comedy + relatability.

---

## Module 2: THE PROBLEM (3–10 seconds)

**Goal:** Show the problem bleeds into her real social life, not just school.

**Scene (3 quick cuts, ~2 seconds each):**

1. **Video call cut** — She's on a group video call. Friends switch to English mid-conversation. She laughs awkwardly, pretending she understood. Nods along.
2. **Instagram DM cut** — A brand/creator DMs her in English about a collab. She stares at it. Doesn't reply. Closes the app.
3. **Home cut** — Family watching an English web series. Everyone laughing at a joke. She laughs 2 seconds late, after watching others laugh first.

**Voiceover (her voice, Telugu):**

> *"Understand అవుతున్నట్టు act చేయడం... చాలా tired గా ఉంది."*
> *(Pretending to understand... I'm so tired of it.)*

**Why this works:**
No school. Pure social life. Three scenarios that happen at home and online — far more relatable to her daily Instagram audience. The voiceover line is honest and vulnerable — that's what stops people from scrolling.

**Shoot tip:** The video call scene can be faked with a split-screen. DM scene — film over-the-shoulder of the phone. Web series scene — dark room, TV glow on her face.

---

## Module 3: THE TURN / SOLUTION (10–20 seconds)

**Goal:** Show the app as a daily habit that fits naturally into her existing routine.

**Scene:**
She's lying on her bed scrolling Instagram. She pauses. Opens **Telugu Daily**.

Screen shows:

- A sentence she's seen before in real life (something like *"That's so funny"* / *"I can't even"*)
- Telugu meaning right below it
- She grins — *"Ohh! అది అర్థమే!"*

**Fast montage (1 second each):**

| Moment       | Visual                                                               |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Morning      | Getting ready, phone propped against mirror, reads 5 sentences       |
| Evening chai | Sitting on terrace, breezy, phone in hand, casual scroll through app |
| Night        | Dark room, low brightness, finishes day's lesson, taps ✅ Done       |

**On-screen counter animates:**

> Day 1 → Day 15 → Day 30 🔥

**Why this works:**
Mirror, terrace, bedroom — these are HER spaces. No effort, no pressure. Just existing + learning. The "Ohh! అది అర్థమే!" moment is the emotional click every learner wants to feel.

**Shoot tip:** Mirror scene is highly shareable and native to Instagram aesthetic. Make sure app screen is readable. Counter animation adds gamification appeal to younger audience.

---

## Module 4: THE PAYOFF (20–28 seconds)

**Goal:** Show transformation inside her social world — no classroom needed.

**Scene:**
Same video call from Module 2. Friends switch to English again.

This time → **she jumps in. In English. Naturally.**

Friends' faces: *"Wait... since when?!"*
She laughs — genuinely this time. Not 2 seconds late.

**Cut to:** She's recording a Reel again. Same energy. Now she replies to the English comment — in English — casually, confidently.

**Her voiceover:**

> *"ఇప్పుడు నేను late గా నవ్వను."*
> *(Now I don't laugh late.)*

**Why this works:**
"నేను late గా నవ్వను" is the kind of line people screenshot and share. It's funny, relatable, and captures the entire emotional journey of the ad in one sentence. No school. Pure social payoff.

**Shoot tip:** The video call payoff should use the exact same background/frame as Module 2 for visual continuity. The Reel-recording-herself ending is meta — she's on Instagram, watching an Instagram ad, about a girl making Instagram content. Deeply native.

---

## Module 5: THE CALL TO ACTION (28–30 seconds)

**Goal:** One clear action.

**Scene:**
App logo. Clean background. Tagline fades in.

**On screen:**

> **Telugu Daily**
> "రోజూ నేర్చుకో, నమ్మకంగా మాట్లాడు"
> *(Learn every day, speak with confidence)*
>
> ✅ Free లో Download చేసుకో 👇

**Why this works:**
Tagline changed from Script 1. Here it's *"నమ్మకంగా మాట్లాడు"* (speak with confidence) — directly addressing the social confidence pain shown in this ad vs. the academic pain in Script 1.

## Full 30-Second Timeline

| Time    | Module   | What's happening                                                                                    |
| ------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0–3s   | Hook     | Girl reads English comment, opens Google Translate — confused                                      |

| 10–20s | Solution | App on bed/mirror/terrace + "Ohh అది అర్థమే!" + day counter                                |
| 20–28s | Payoff   | Video call — she speaks first now + replies to English comment + "నేను late గా నవ్వను" |
| 28–30s | CTA      | Logo + "నమ్మకంగా మాట్లాడు" + Free Download                                          |

## How Script 2 Differs from Script 1

|          | Script 1                                | Script 2                                       |
| -------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Setting  | Classroom, school bus, bedroom          | Home, video call, Instagram, terrace           |
| Pain     | Academic embarrassment                  | Social / online embarrassment                  |
| Payoff   | Answers teacher in class                | Joins friend conversation + replies to comment |
| Tone     | Inspirational                           | Relatable + slightly funny                     |
| Key line | "రోజూ 10 నిమిషాలు చాలు" | "నేను late గా నవ్వను"              |
| Best for | Students (15–18)                       | Content creators, social teens (16–22)        |

Run both as A/B test on Instagram Ads to see which converts better.

- [ ] Bedroom (main location)

- [ ] Shoot vertical 9:16
- [ ] Bedroom lighting: fairy lights / warm lamp — Instagram aesthetic
- [ ] Terrace scene: golden hour if possible (5–6 PM)
- [ ] Subtitles for all Telugu voiceover (mute watchers)
