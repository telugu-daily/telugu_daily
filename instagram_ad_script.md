# Telugu Daily — Instagram Ad Script
## "అర్థమైందా?" (Did you understand?)
**Format:** Instagram Reel / Story | **Duration:** 30 seconds | **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (Vertical)

---

## Module 1: THE HOOK (0–3 seconds)
**Goal:** Stop the scroll in the first 2 seconds.

**Scene:**
Close-up of a teenage girl's face. She's reading a text on her phone in English. Her expression = frozen, embarrassed, confused.

**Text overlay (on screen):**
> "నీకు ఇలా అనిపించిందా?"
> *(Ever felt like this?)*

**Why this works:**
Instagram shows the first 2–3 seconds before giving a "skip" option. The girl's face mirrors a real emotion every Telugu teenager has felt — the fear of not understanding English. Viewers instantly relate and keep watching.

**Shoot tip:** Film in extreme close-up. Eyes and expression must be visible. No text, no logo yet.

---

## Module 2: THE PROBLEM (3–10 seconds)
**Goal:** Amplify the pain. Make the viewer nod and say "yes, that's me."

**Scene (3 quick cuts, ~2 seconds each):**

1. **Classroom cut** — Teacher asks a question in English. Girl looks down. Silence. Classmates look at her.
2. **Group chat cut** — Phone screen shows a group chat in English. She reads it. Doesn't reply. Types and deletes.
3. **Friend cut** — Friend laughs and says *"Arre, English lo cheppu na!"* → Girl's face falls.

**Voiceover (her internal voice, in Telugu):**
> *"English వస్తే life different గా ఉంటుందని తెలుసు... కానీ ఎక్కడ మొదలుపెట్టాలో తెలీదు."*
> *(I know life would be different if I knew English... but I don't know where to start.)*

**Why this works:**
Three scenarios = three types of viewers covered (students, social media users, friend groups). The internal voiceover in Telugu makes it feel like *her* story, not an ad. Viewers feel understood.

**Shoot tip:** Real locations only — actual classroom, real phone screen, natural friend interaction. No studio backgrounds.

---

## Module 3: THE TURN / SOLUTION (10–20 seconds)
**Goal:** Introduce the app as the natural answer — not as a sales pitch.

**Scene:**
Same girl, now sitting relaxed (bedroom / balcony / chai moment). She opens **Telugu Daily** on her phone. Camera shows screen briefly:
- Telugu sentence on top
- English translation below
- Simple, clean UI

Her face changes — *"Oh... ఇది నాకు అర్థమవుతోంది!"* (This makes sense to me!)

**Fast montage (1 second each cut):**
| Moment | Visual |
|---|---|
| Morning | She's drinking chai, phone in hand, reads 5 sentences |
| School bus | Earphones in, scrolling through the app |
| Night | Lying in bed, completes the day's lesson, taps ✅ |

**On-screen counter animates:**
> Day 1 → Day 7 → Day 30

**Why this works:**
The montage shows the app fits into *existing* daily habits — no extra effort needed. The day counter creates a sense of progress and momentum. Showing real app UI builds trust.

**Shoot tip:** Film phone screen with a second phone or screen recorder. Keep app UI visible for at least 2 full seconds so viewers can read it.

---

## Module 4: THE PAYOFF (20–28 seconds)
**Goal:** Show the transformation. Deliver the emotional reward the viewer has been waiting for.

**Scene:**
Same classroom. Same teacher. Same type of question — in English.

This time → **she answers. Clearly. Confidently. In English.**

Friend's jaw drops. Teacher nods approvingly. Girl smiles — not a big dramatic smile, just quiet confidence.

**Her voiceover:**
> *"రోజూ 10 నిమిషాలు చాలు."*
> *(Just 10 minutes a day is enough.)*

**Why this works:**
This is the payoff for sitting through the ad. The viewer sees the before and after in the same setting — same classroom, same girl, completely different outcome. The line "రోజూ 10 నిమిషాలు చాలు" is the single most important message of the entire ad. It removes the biggest objection: *"I don't have time."*

**Shoot tip:** Use the exact same location, same outfit color as Module 2 classroom scene so viewers subconsciously connect the two moments.

---

## Module 5: THE CALL TO ACTION (28–30 seconds)
**Goal:** Tell the viewer exactly what to do next.

**Scene:**
Clean screen — app logo center frame. Tagline animates in.

**On screen:**
> **Telugu Daily**
> "రోజూ నేర్చుకో, జీవితం మార్చుకో"
> *(Learn every day, change your life)*
>
> ✅ Free లో Download చేసుకో 👇
> [App Store / Play Store badge]

**Why this works:**
After an emotional journey, the viewer is primed to act. The CTA is simple — one action (download), one benefit (free), one place to look (link in bio / swipe up). No confusion.

**Shoot tip:** Keep this screen for exactly 2 seconds. Add a subtle "ping" or notification sound effect when the app logo appears — triggers familiarity.

---

## Full 30-Second Timeline

| Time | Module | What's happening |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Hook | Girl's confused face + "నీకు ఇలా అనిపించిందా?" |
| 3–10s | Problem | 3 embarrassing scenes + internal Telugu voiceover |
| 10–20s | Solution | App on phone + daily habit montage + day counter |
| 20–28s | Payoff | She answers confidently in class + "రోజూ 10 నిమిషాలు చాలు" |
| 28–30s | CTA | App logo + tagline + "Free లో Download చేసుకో" |

## Production Checklist

### Cast
- [ ] Telugu teenage girl (15–18), natural look, no heavy makeup
- [ ] 1–2 friends for group scenes
- [ ] Teacher (can be off-camera voice only)

### Locations
- [ ] Classroom or study room
- [ ] School bus or auto
- [ ] Bedroom / home balcony

### Technical
- [ ] Shoot vertical 9:16 (phone held portrait)
- [ ] Natural lighting preferred over studio lights
- [ ] Subtitles for all Telugu voiceover (many watch Reels on mute)
- [ ] Add trending Telugu BGM (low volume, under voiceover)
- [ ] Total file size under 100 MB for Instagram upload
